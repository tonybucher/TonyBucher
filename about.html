About me

I have been pursuing creative arts for over 5 years in graphic design, web design, photography, and videography. I have spent the bulk of my youth providing different teams, ministries, and friends with logos, graphics and videos that engage our community. Lately, I have been fortunate enough to teach high schoolers these creative skills so that they can develop in their own passions of creativity.

Currently, I am graduated from college and am taking steps out of my comfort zone by reaching outside of my immediate community in hopes of being a well known multimedia designer.

There is something so fulfilling when I am able to create content for somebody that exceeds their expectation!

I would love to connect!

If you have any questions or if you're interested in grabbing a cup of coffee, I would love to chat!
